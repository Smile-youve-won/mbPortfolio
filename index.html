About

I started my professional career by completing an apprenticeship and earning a HNC in electrical engineering whilst working on the protection and control systems for offshore windfarms.

After my apprenticeship I moved into a service and support role where I was an on-call engineer supporting National Grid substations across the country.

From there I transferred my skills into industrial automation where I learned how to program PLCs working in many different industries such as the rail industry and water utilities.

During this time I started to teach myself HTML, CSS and JavaScript though various courses on Udemy, YouTube and other online resources.

I have always had a passion for design, creativity and learning how things work; as a result have found myself developing a keen interest for front end web development, where I hope to put my new found skills to work.

Outside of this, I have a stong love for outdoor adventures which will normally evolve around climbing, surfing, and travelling in my van with my partner.
